E-commerce Profit Leakage Audit
Project Overview
I performed a strategic audit of nearly 10,000 sales records to identify where aggressive discounting was eroding bottom-line margins. By calculating a custom "Max Discount" threshold for each product, I identified a 54.52% profit recovery potential.

Key Insights
The Furniture Trap: Furniture generates high revenue but the lowest profit due to an average discount of 26% vs. a 19% break-even point.
Regional Variance: The Central region showed the highest "leakage," primarily due to logistics costs not being factored into pricing.
Recovery Strategy: Implementing a 6.8% revenue-based recovery cap would stabilize margins across all regions.

Tools Used
SQL: Data cleaning and complex break-even calculations.
Advanced Excel: Pivot modeling, Power Pivot (DAX), and interactive dashboarding.
